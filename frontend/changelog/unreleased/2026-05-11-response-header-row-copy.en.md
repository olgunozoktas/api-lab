---
title: Click to copy individual response header values
date: 2026-05-11
---

Each row in the response **Headers** tab now reveals a small copy
button on hover. Click it to put just that header's value on the
clipboard — useful for grabbing `Authorization`, `Location`,
`Set-Cookie`, `X-Request-Id` and friends without text-selecting
through wrapped lines or picking the line out of the raw view.

A toast confirms which header was copied so accidental clicks aren't
silent.
