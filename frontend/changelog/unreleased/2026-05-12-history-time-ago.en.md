---
title: History rows now show how long ago each request ran
date: 2026-05-12
---

Each history entry now carries a small muted relative-time chip
("3m", "2h", "5d") right before the elapsed-time + status pills.
Hover the chip for the exact timestamp.

Combined with v0.2.10's timing chip and v0.2.11's status code chip,
the right edge of every history row now answers three questions at
a glance: **when** did it run, **how fast** was it, and **what** did
it return.
