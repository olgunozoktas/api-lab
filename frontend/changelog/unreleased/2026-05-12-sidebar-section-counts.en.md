---
title: Sidebar section headers show item counts
date: 2026-05-12
---

The two sidebar section headers — **SAVED** and **RECENT REQUESTS** —
now carry a small count pill right after the title:

- **SAVED `12`** — number of saved requests across all folders
- **RECENT REQUESTS `47`** — number of history entries (capped at 200)

Matches the count badges the Environments modal and env switcher
gained recently. Lets you glance at how much is in the list without
opening the section.
