---
title: ⌘B toggles the sidebar
date: 2026-05-11
---

Press **⌘B** anywhere to collapse the saved-requests + history
sidebar, freeing the full window width for the composer and response
viewer. Press again to bring it back. State persists across launches.

Matches the primary-sidebar shortcut used by VS Code, Cursor, Linear
and most editor-style apps. Useful when you're focused on a single
request and the sidebar list is just visual noise.
