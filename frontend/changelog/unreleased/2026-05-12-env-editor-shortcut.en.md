---
title: ⌘+Shift+E opens the Environments editor
date: 2026-05-12
---

Press **⌘+Shift+E** anywhere — even while typing in an input — to
open the Environments editor modal. The "Env..." button in the top
bar shows the `⌘⇧E` hint in its tooltip, and the shortcut joins the
rest in Settings → Shortcuts.

Shift-prefixed so it doesn't collide with WebKit's native ⌘+E
("Use Selection for Find") that's still useful inside text fields.

Three top-bar destinations are now one keystroke away:
**⌘,** for Settings · **⌘⇧E** for Environments · **?** for Guides.
