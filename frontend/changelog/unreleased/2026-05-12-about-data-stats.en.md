---
title: Settings → About: "Your data" stats grid
date: 2026-05-12
---

The About section in Settings now shows a small five-tile grid with
live counts of the data API Lab has stored locally for you:

- **Requests** — saved request items across all folders
- **Folders** — collection folders you've created
- **History** — recent entries (capped at the last 200)
- **Environments** — distinct envs in the switcher
- **Examples** — saved response examples across all requests

Gives you a quick "how much have I built up here" snapshot —
particularly useful before flipping `--reset-state` or wiping
WebKit's local storage.
