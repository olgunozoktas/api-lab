---
title: Undo a deleted collection item
date: 2026-05-19
---

Deleting a request or folder from the sidebar now raises a toast with
an **Undo** button. Click it and the item — or the whole folder
subtree, with its expansion state — comes straight back, exactly where
it was. The toast sticks around a little longer than usual so you have
time to react.

No more "did I just lose that?" — a mis-click is one click to reverse.
