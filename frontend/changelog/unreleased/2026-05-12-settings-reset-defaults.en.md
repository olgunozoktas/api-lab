---
title: Reset to defaults button under Settings → Request defaults
date: 2026-05-12
---

Tweaked `Timeout`, `Max redirects` or `Skip TLS verification` and want
to undo it? A small **Reset to defaults** button now appears under the
Request defaults section whenever any field differs from the shipped
baseline — and disappears once you're back on defaults. No need to
remember the original values.
