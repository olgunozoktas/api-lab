---
title: Active env name is always visible in the top bar
date: 2026-05-12
---

The env switcher dropdown still hides when you only have one
environment (no point picking from a one-item list), but the
**active env name + var count** now shows as a read-only chip in
its place. So you can always answer "which env is active right now?"
without opening the Env… modal.

When you create a second environment, the chip is replaced by the
existing dropdown — same data, click to switch.
