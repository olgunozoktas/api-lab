title: Five more integrations — GitHub, OpenAI, Slack, Notion, Linear
date: 2026-05-18
---

The Integrations gallery grows from two providers to seven. Alongside
Cloudflare and Stripe, you can now enable:

- **GitHub** — repos, issues, pull requests, actions
- **OpenAI** — chat completions, models, embeddings, images, audio
- **Slack** — messages, conversations, users, files
- **Notion** — pages, databases, blocks, search
- **Linear** — its GraphQL endpoint, opened ready in the GraphQL composer

Each lands as a curated, ready-to-use collection with auth
pre-scaffolded — just drop in your token and send.
