---
title: Right-click any history entry for quick actions
date: 2026-05-11
---

The history sidebar now opens a context menu on right-click with four
actions:

- **Replay** — load the request into the current tab and re-send (same
  as left-click).
- **Open in new tab** — load into a fresh tab, keeping the current one
  untouched. Great for side-by-side comparison: original on the left,
  retry on the right.
- **Copy URL** — copy the request URL to the clipboard.
- **Delete** — remove just this one entry (the "Clear" button still
  nukes everything).

Pairs nicely with the new status filter pills: narrow to **5xx**,
right-click → **Open in new tab** to investigate without losing the
request you were working on.
