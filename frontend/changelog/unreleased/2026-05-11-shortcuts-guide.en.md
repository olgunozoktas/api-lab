A consolidated reference covering every shortcut shipped so far —
sending, tabs, navigation, composer paste, and a few "right-click for
more" tips — lives under the Workspace group of the in-app Guide hub
(press `?` to open). Comes in both English and Turkish.

Previously, shortcuts were scattered across other guides and discovered
mostly by accident. They're now one click away.
