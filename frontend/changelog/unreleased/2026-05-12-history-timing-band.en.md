---
title: History rows show color-coded elapsed time
date: 2026-05-12
---

Each history entry in the sidebar now carries a small `Xms` chip
next to the status dot, coloured with the same perceptual bands the
response header uses (green <200 ms, muted to 700 ms, orange to 2 s,
red beyond). Hover the chip to see the band label.

Scanning the history list now answers "which past calls were slow?"
without having to replay them — fast/slow is visible in one glance,
same visual language as the live response pane.
