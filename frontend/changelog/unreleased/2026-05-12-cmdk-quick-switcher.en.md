---
title: ⌘ K now opens the quick switcher (alongside ⌘ P)
date: 2026-05-12
---

⌘ K is the muscle-memory shortcut for the command-palette /
quick-switcher in modern apps — Linear, Notion, Slack, GitHub, Raycast.
The quick switcher already responded to ⌘ P (the Postman convention);
it now responds to **⌘ K** too.

No behavior change beyond the keybinding — same fuzzy search, same
tabs / collections sections. Settings → Keyboard shortcuts and the
in-app guide both show `⌘ K / ⌘ P` now.
