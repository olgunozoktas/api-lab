---
title: ⌘+, opens Settings, matching the macOS convention
date: 2026-05-12
---

The macOS-standard Preferences shortcut now works app-wide:
press **⌘+,** anywhere — even while typing in the URL bar or a
body editor — and the Settings modal opens. The Settings button
tooltip shows the `⌘,` hint next to the label, and the shortcut
is listed in Settings → Shortcuts alongside the rest.

Closes the last gap with the other top-bar entry points (the
Guides icon already had `?`; the version chip already opens the
changelog). Every top-bar destination is now one keystroke away.
