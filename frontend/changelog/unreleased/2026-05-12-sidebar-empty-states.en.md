---
title: Richer empty states for the Collections + History sidebars
date: 2026-05-12
---

A blank sidebar used to greet new users with a single terse line —
"No saved requests yet" or "No history yet". Both sidebars now lead
with that line as a heading and follow with 2–3 actionable bullets:

**Collections** — three concrete ways to populate it: send + ⌘ S to
save, right-click + Folder → New request, or use the Import button
for a Postman v2 export.

**History** — explains the auto-populate behavior, that the last
200 requests are kept locally, click vs right-click load behaviour,
and the status filter pills above the list.

Same explainer pattern the composer-tab hints introduced — keep the
guidance right where the user is looking when they have a question.
