---
title: ⌘ L focuses + selects the URL bar
date: 2026-05-11
---

Press `⌘ L` to jump straight to the URL bar and select its current
contents — the same address-bar shortcut every modern browser uses.
Type to replace the URL, or hit `→` to position the caret at the end.

Saves a trip to the trackpad when iterating on endpoint paths during
a debug session. The shortcut now appears in the full keyboard
reference under **Settings → Keyboard shortcuts**.
